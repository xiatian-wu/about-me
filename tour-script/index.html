Video Tour Script

Access the interactive tour at any page by adding ?tour=1 to the URL, e.g. https://xiatian-wu.github.io/about-me/?tour=1

Step 1 — Welcome (About page)

English

Hi, I'm Xiatian Iogansen — a transportation researcher at George Washington University and NIST. This site is a hub for my research, CV, and publications. Let me walk you through the main sections.

中文

大家好，我是Xiatian Iogansen，目前在乔治华盛顿大学和美国国家标准与技术研究院从事交通研究。这个网站是我的学术主页，包含我的研究方向、简历和论文列表。让我带大家简单浏览一下。

Step 2 — About Page

English

The About page introduces my background, current affiliation, and research focus. My CV timeline below shows my academic and work history — all rendered automatically from a single YAML data file, so I only need to update one place.

中文

About页面介绍了我的学术背景、所在机构和研究方向。下方的简历时间轴展示了我的学习和工作经历——这些内容由一个YAML数据文件自动渲染，只需更新一处即可同步显示。

Step 3 — CV & Links (About page)

English

At the top you'll find links to my CV PDF, email, Google Scholar, and GitHub profiles. The CV section below lists my positions and education with dates and descriptions — no HTML editing required to maintain it.

中文

页面顶部有我的CV下载链接、邮箱、Google Scholar和GitHub主页。下方的简历部分按时间列出了我的职位和教育经历，维护起来非常方便，不需要直接编辑HTML。

Step 4 — Research Graph (Research page)

English

This is the Research page. The interactive graph shows how my research topics and papers are connected — inspired by the knowledge graph in Obsidian. Nodes are color-coded: purple for topics, green for published, yellow for under review, gray for in preparation.

中文

这是Research（研究）页面。交互式图谱展示了我的研究主题和论文之间的关联——灵感来自Obsidian的知识图谱。节点按颜色分类：紫色为研究主题，绿色为已发表，黄色为审稿中，灰色为准备中。

Step 5 — Graph Interactions (Research page)

English

Try it: hover any node to see a tooltip with details. Click a topic node — like "EV Adoption" — to jump straight to that section in the list view below. Click a paper node to open the publication.

中文

可以试试：将鼠标悬停在节点上，可以看到详细信息的提示框。点击研究主题节点（如"EV Adoption"）会直接跳转到下方列表视图中对应的部分。点击论文节点则会打开该论文链接。

Step 6 — List View & Drafts (Research page)

English

Switch to List View to browse papers by topic. Papers under review or in preparation are marked with badges. Some have a Draft button — click it to download the working paper before it's formally published.

中文

切换到列表视图可以按主题浏览论文。审稿中或准备中的论文会标注状态标签。部分论文还有Draft（草稿）按钮，点击即可在正式发表前下载工作论文。

Step 7 — Blog (Blog page)

English

The Blog documents how this site was built — entirely through natural-language instructions to Claude Code, an AI coding assistant, with no prior web development experience. If you're an academic thinking about building your own site, this post walks through the full process.

中文

Blog页面记录了这个网站的搭建过程——完全通过自然语言指令与AI编程助手Claude Code协作完成，全程无需网页开发基础。如果你也想搭建自己的学术主页，这篇文章详细介绍了整个流程。
